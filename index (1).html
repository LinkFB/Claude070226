Why Link Fort Bend

Local energy, organized.

Fort Bend County is one of the most diverse communities in the country — and home to no shortage of people ready to act. Link Fort Bend exists to make sure that energy reaches the organizations and campaigns that need it most, instead of getting lost.

Local first

Every opportunity we list is rooted in Fort Bend County — no national form letters, just neighbors organizing for neighbors.

Low barrier to entry

Whatever time or skills you have to offer, there's a place for you. We match, you show up.

Part of something bigger

Link Fort Bend is part of the nationwide Indivisible network of local, volunteer-led groups.

Built to last

We're organizing for the long haul — building durable relationships between volunteers and local groups.
